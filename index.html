Welcome to ZOOQUEST.
The instructions are simple: For each scenario, just pick which option you'd like to do, and follow the story through to the end... Whatever that end may be!
There are 4 ways to 'win' and earn a point, but 10 ways to 'lose'. After each win/lose you will return to the start of the day.

Feeling competitive? Screenshot your score and send this game to your friends. I've even included a button to reset your score, in case you feel the need to hide your shame of losing so many times from your friends. Aren't I nice person? Now go have fun at the zoo!
